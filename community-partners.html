Thank You for Making Recovery Possible

A Heartfelt Thank You

Recovery NM exists because of the generosity and compassion of our community partners, donors, and supporters. Your contributions directly fund scholarships that give New Mexico residents access to life-changing substance abuse treatment.

Every donation, whether large or small, represents hope, healing, and a second chance at life. Together, we are building a stronger, healthier New Mexico—one person, one family, one community at a time.

Thank you for believing in our mission and for standing with those who need it most.
