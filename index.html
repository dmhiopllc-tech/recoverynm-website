Recovery NM

Financial Assistance for Substance Abuse Treatment in New Mexico

Recovery NM is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making substance abuse treatment accessible for New Mexico residents. Our mission is to provide financial support through scholarships, ensuring that no one is denied access to life-changing treatment due to financial limitations.

By connecting individuals with the addiction treatment centers best suited to their needs, we are actively working to reduce the impact of addiction on families and communities. Every donation we receive goes directly toward these scholarships, creating a pathway to healing and lasting recovery in our home state. Together, we can build a stronger, healthier New Mexico.

Trusted Partnerships for Addiction Treatment in New Mexico

Building Pathways to Recovery Through Trusted Partnerships

At Recovery NM, we believe that effective substance abuse treatment begins with trusted relationships. We partner closely with a network of established addiction treatment providers across New Mexico to ensure every individual seeking recovery receives the highest quality care.

By leveraging these partnerships, we can connect those in urgent need with the programs and services best suited to their unique journey. These relationships enable us to offer resources that lead to more personalized and effective treatment options, ultimately increasing the chances of long-term recovery.

Recovery NM is committed to providing not only financial assistance but also a foundation of trust, expertise, and compassion in every step of the recovery process. Together with our partners, we are working toward a healthier, more hopeful New Mexico.
